Thanksgiving Banner Demo

This page demonstrates the new Thanksgiving banner with falling leaves animation.

The banner should appear at the top, and after a couple seconds, you'll see a beautiful falling leaves animation overlay the entire screen.

The animation sequence:

  1. Banner loads with Thanksgiving message
  2. After 1.5 seconds, falling leaves animation begins
  3. Animation runs for 6 seconds with leaves falling from top to bottom
  4. Animation fades out smoothly
